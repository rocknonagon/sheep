Buzz

A story about a sheep who didn't grow as much wool as the other sheep

By Lucy Gow

About this page.

You're not worth the grass you gobble!

On Bluster Ridge farm, the sheep grew round

And up and out and down with wool.

When Buzz grew bristles and clumpy tufts

The other sheep were cruel.

where the bully was gone,

the sheep were shorn,

the crocodile fell in a well,

and Buzz had a story to tell.

So Buzz left the farm.

But where would he go?

How would he live?

He didn't know.

All the way back to the farm...

Miaow!

What lovely fur!

Oh, what I could do with fur like that!

Along the way he met a cat.

back past the cat.

Aak!

My what lovely feathers!

Oh, what I could do with feathers like that!

He met a bird.

back past the bird

Grrr!

My what a lovely tail!

Oh, what I could do with a tail like that!

And then a fox.

back past the fox.

My what lovely scales!

Oh, what I could do with scales like that!

Snap!

GO BACK!

Finally he met a crocodile...

and the crocodile chased him
