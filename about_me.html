About me

I'm a stay-at-home mum and I like reading picture books to my son.

So I guess I just have picture books in my mind and wanted to try to make a website that tells a story.

If you move from the first page across to the right, and hover over the top half of each page, an image of Buzz the sheep should appear, with a dialogue. As you move past the animals, they turn to you. Then once you reach the end, the previous panels change. Then if you go back the crocodile should follow you.

Hit the reset button to begin the story again (this is implemented in JavaScript).

There's definitely some things I would change about the page if I had more time, but I hope it works okay for you. Thanks.
